## Authorization Required

**You may ONLY use OpenClaw Security against:**

1. Systems you own
2. Systems for which you have **explicit written authorization** from the owner
3. Designated training environments (e.g., OWASP Juice Shop, HackTheBox, TryHackMe)

**Unauthorized access to computer systems is illegal** under:
- Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) — United States
- Computer Misuse Act — United Kingdom
- Similar laws in most jurisdictions worldwide

Penalties include criminal prosecution, fines, and imprisonment.

## User Responsibility

By using OpenClaw Security, you acknowledge and agree that:

1. **You are solely responsible** for ensuring you have proper authorization before testing any target
